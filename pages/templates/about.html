Welcome to Restock

The best place to get notifications for in demand items.

Restock was created to help users get notified and informed when in demand items are back in stock. Our goal is to help our users get the items they want without wasting time searching and navigating through various websites. As of right now we are tracking Graphics cards and consoles from various vendors such as Adorama, Amazon, BestBuy, GameStop, MicroCenter and B&H. When a user clicks on a product from a specific vendor they will be introduce to the price, the reviews, the stock availabilty, the url link, and a graph showing stock trends of the product. Hopefully with the information we provide to our users they will purchase any item they are looking for.
