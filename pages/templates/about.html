Welcome to Restock

The best place to get notifications for in demand items.

● The vision of Restock is to allow our users to be informed on the sought-after products they are looking forward to purchasing
● The target audience of Restock are people who are looking for high in demand items. In demand items are items that get sold out
in minutes and we want to give our users the ability to quickly purchase after being notified.
● Our strategy is to create a website where users can see various products and their vendors with price, availability,
rating, and url for the product, as well as the ability to be notified for their desired items. In addition, users can view graphs showing more information on the item.
● Our goal is to help our users get the items they want without wasting time searching and navigating through various websites
